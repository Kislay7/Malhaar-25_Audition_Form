About the Festival

Get ready for the ultimate experience! Our most awaited fests bring together music, dance, drama, and more, creating unforgettable memories each year!

Department Descriptions

Sponsorship: The backbone of the fest's financial support! You'll negotiate with companies and organizations to secure funding, partnerships, and resources.

Events: Be the mastermind behind the fun! Plan, organize, and execute various activities and performances.

Public Relations: Be the voice of MALHAAR, managing communication, promotion, and outreach.

Technical: Handle everything tech-related, including event registrations and website management.

Graphics: Create eye-catching designs for posters, banners, and social media. Knowledge of reel-making preferred.

Content Writing: Craft compelling narratives for social media posts, promotional content, and event descriptions.

Decor: Transform venues with creative decorations that match the spirit of MALHAAR.

Logistics: Ensure all materials, equipment, and people are in the right place at the right time.

Security: Maintain a safe and orderly environment during the fest.
